Eco Heroes

Meet the visionary leaders and changemakers driving sustainability and environmental protection worldwide.

Activist

Greta Thunberg

Swedish climate activist who sparked a global youth climate movement through school strikes for climate action.

Naturalist

David Attenborough

Legendary broadcaster and naturalist inspiring millions through documentaries about nature and environmental conservation.

Visionary

Wangari Maathai

Kenyan environmental and political activist who founded the Green Belt Movement for tree planting and conservation.

Pioneer

Bill Mollison

Australian visionary who pioneered permaculture as a sustainable approach to agriculture and land management.

Author

Rachel Carson

Pioneer environmentalist whose writings sparked the global environmental movement and conservation efforts.

Scientist

Dr. Vandana Shiva

Indian scholar and environmental activist leading the movement for seed saving and biodiversity protection.

Philanthropist

Leonardo DiCaprio

Actor and philanthropist advocating for climate action and supporting environmental conservation projects globally.

Scientist

Jane Goodall

Primatologist and conservationist promoting wildlife protection and sustainable living through her institute.

Environmentalist

Sunita Narain

Indian environmentalist and director of Centre for Science and Environment working on pollution and sustainability.
