Sustainability Organizations by Country

Explore environmental organizations from the United States, United Kingdom, Australia, and other nations driving sustainability worldwide.

EPA

Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)

United States federal agency responsible for environmental protection. It develops environmental policies, enforces pollution control laws, and manages air quality, water quality, and hazardous waste programs nationwide.

NOAA

National Oceanic & Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)

U.S. agency responsible for ocean and atmospheric science. It manages marine sanctuaries, conducts climate research, monitors ocean health, and coordinates national climate adaptation strategies.

USFWS

United States Fish & Wildlife Service (USFWS)

Federal service dedicated to wildlife conservation. It manages national wildlife refuges, protects endangered species, restores habitats, and implements conservation programs across the United States.

EA

Environment Agency (United Kingdom)

UK government agency responsible for environmental protection and flood defense. It regulates waste, water, and air quality while coordinating climate adaptation across England and Wales.

NE

Natural England

Government agency dedicated to nature conservation in England. It manages protected sites, protects habitats, advises on biodiversity, and coordinates species-recovery programs nationwide.

RSPB

Royal Society for the Protection of Birds (RSPB)

UK's largest wildlife conservation organization focused on avian protection. It manages nature reserves, conducts bird research, and runs community engagement programs across the United Kingdom.

DCCEEW

Department of Climate Change, Energy & Environment (Australia)

Australian government department responsible for climate policy and environmental protection. It implements carbon-reduction strategies, manages renewable-energy programs, and coordinates national sustainability initiatives.

CSIRO

Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (CSIRO)

Australia's national science agency conducting environmental research. It focuses on climate science, sustainable agriculture, marine conservation, and environmental solutions affecting millions.

GBRMPA

Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Authority

Authority dedicated to protecting the Great Barrier Reef. It manages the world's largest coral reef system, conducts coral research, monitors reef health, and implements marine conservation strategies.
