International Sustainability Organizations

Discover leading global organizations driving environmental protection and sustainable development worldwide.

UNEP

United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP)

Leading global authority on environmental issues. It coordinates environmental policy across member states and addresses climate change, biodiversity loss, and pollution while supporting sustainable development agendas.

UNDP

United Nations Development Programme (UNDP)

Global development organization connecting countries to knowledge, experience, and resources. It implements climate adaptation, renewable-energy, and sustainable-development projects that affect millions of people.

IPCC

Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC)

Scientific body assessing climate change and its impacts. It synthesizes peer-reviewed research to provide governments with authoritative climate information that informs global policy.

WWF

World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF)

Global conservation organization with decades of impact. It works in more than 100 countries to protect endangered species, forests, oceans, and freshwater ecosystems through science-based initiatives.

IUCN

International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN)

Global union of governments, NGOs, and experts providing scientific expertise on biodiversity. It publishes the Red List of Threatened Species and influences conservation policy around the world.

GP

Greenpeace International

Independent global campaign organization dedicated to environmental protection. It runs high-impact campaigns focused on climate change, ocean conservation, forest protection, and clean energy.

CI

Conservation International

Organization protecting Earth's biodiversity hotspots. It partners with indigenous communities and governments to safeguard forests, marine ecosystems, and sustainable livelihoods in critical regions.

BL

BirdLife International

Global partnership of conservation organizations focused on avian conservation. It manages protected areas, conducts research on threatened bird species, and advocates for biodiversity protection policies worldwide.
