Indian Sustainability Organizations

Explore the leading Indian organizations driving environmental protection and sustainable development initiatives across the nation.

MOEFCC

Ministry of Environment, Forest & Climate Change

The apex body responsible for environmental protection and climate policy in India. It formulates national policies on pollution control, forest management, and sustainable development initiatives.

CPCB

Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB)

Statutory body tasked with regulating industrial pollution and maintaining environmental standards. It monitors air and water quality, enforces pollution control norms, and coordinates with state agencies.

NMCG

National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG)

Flagship program dedicated to rejuvenating the Ganga River. It implements pollution control projects, waste-management systems, and biodiversity conservation efforts along the river basin affecting 400 million people.

WII

Wildlife Institute of India (WII)

Premier research organization dedicated to wildlife conservation and management. It conducts research on endangered species and supports protected areas and biodiversity hotspots across India's ecosystems.

NBA

National Biodiversity Authority (NBA)

Constitutional body established under the Biological Diversity Act. It works to conserve Indian biodiversity, protect traditional knowledge, and support the sustainable use of biological resources.

WWF

WWF India

Leading conservation NGO working in India since 1956. It focuses on tiger conservation, marine protection, habitat restoration, and climate change mitigation across multiple Indian states.

WTI

Wildlife Trust of India (WTI)

NGO pioneering wildlife conservation and biodiversity protection. It works on saving endangered species from extinction, managing protected areas, and partnering with local communities for grassroots conservation.

BNHS

Bombay Natural History Society (BNHS)

India's oldest research organization dedicated to natural sciences and wildlife conservation. It conducts biodiversity surveys, ornithological studies, and conservation education across the subcontinent.

TERI

The Energy Resources Institute (TERI)

Research institution focused on energy, environment, and sustainable development. It conducts work on renewable energy, climate action, and practical sustainability solutions that inform national policy.
